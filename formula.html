What makes

Every major energy drink is built around caffeine as the primary energy source. Altus replaces caffeine with a stack of natural adaptogens, B vitamins, and nitric oxide boosters that work with your body — not against it.

Ingredient Breakdown
Every Ingredient

01
Organic Coconut Water
Hydration Base
The foundation of the formula.
Coconut water is one of nature's most effective hydration sources. Rich in potassium, magnesium, and natural electrolytes, it replenishes what you lose during performance without the artificial additives found in traditional sports drinks.
We use organic coconut water concentrate as our primary liquid base — giving Altus its clean, natural electrolyte profile from the very first ingredient.
PotassiumMagnesiumNatural ElectrolytesOrganic
02
Organic Beetroot Extract
Natural Energy
Nature's pre-workout.
Beetroot is one of the most researched natural performance ingredients. It increases nitric oxide production in the body, which dilates blood vessels, improves oxygen delivery to muscles, and enhances endurance — all without caffeine.
Studies show beetroot extract can improve athletic performance by 1–3% — meaningful at every level of sport. It's also what gives Altus Pink Lemonade its naturally vibrant color.
Nitric OxideEnduranceBlood FlowNatural Color
03
Organic Ashwagandha
Adaptogen
Fight fatigue. Not your nerves.
Ashwagandha is an ancient adaptogen used for thousands of years to reduce fatigue, support recovery, and improve physical performance. Unlike caffeine, it doesn't spike and crash — it helps your body adapt to stress and sustain energy over time.
We use KSM-66 ashwagandha — the most researched, bioavailable form of the extract. It's safe for all ages including youth athletes, with no stimulant effect.
Anti-FatigueRecoveryStress ResponseKSM-66
04
L-Theanine
Focus
Calm focus. Zero jitters.
L-Theanine is an amino acid found naturally in green tea that promotes a state of relaxed alertness. It sharpens focus and attention without the anxious, jittery feeling that caffeine causes — making it ideal for athletes who need mental clarity during competition.
When combined with B vitamins, L-Theanine creates a synergistic focus effect. It's completely safe for all ages and is one of the most studied amino acids for cognitive performance.
Mental ClarityNo JittersAmino AcidFocus
05
Vitamin B6 + B12
Energy Metabolism
Real energy from the inside out.
B vitamins are essential for converting food into usable energy at the cellular level. B6 supports protein metabolism and neurotransmitter production, while B12 supports red blood cell formation and reduces fatigue — both critical for athletic performance.
We use methylcobalamin (B12) and pyridoxine HCl (B6) — the most bioavailable forms of each vitamin. These are the same forms used in clinical studies and are safe for all age groups.
Energy MetabolismAnti-FatigueMethylcobalaminAll Ages
06
Organic Lemon + Strawberry
Pink Lemonade Flavor
Flavor the right way.
Organic lemon juice concentrate and Meyer lemon extract form the tart, bright lemonade base. Organic strawberry extract adds natural sweetness and depth — no artificial flavors, no synthetic colors, no "flavor enhancers."
Sweetened with a blend of organic cane sugar and organic monk fruit extract. Low sugar (8g per can), no stevia aftertaste, and a flavor profile that tastes like real lemonade — because it is.
Organic LemonStrawberry ExtractMonk FruitNo Artificial Flavor
07
Electrolyte Complex
Hydration
Replenish what you lose.
Beyond the coconut water base, Altus adds a targeted electrolyte complex to ensure complete hydration support. Pink Himalayan salt provides sodium, potassium citrate supports fluid balance, magnesium citrate aids muscle function, and calcium lactate supports bone health.
The electrolyte profile is calibrated for active performance — enough to replenish sweat loss during training without the excessive sodium found in traditional sports drinks.
