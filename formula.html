What makes
Altus different.

Every major energy drink is built around caffeine. Altus replaces caffeine with a stack of natural adaptogens, B vitamins, and nitric oxide boosters that work with your body — not against it. Real, sustained energy with no spike, no crash, and no ingredients you need to Google.

01
Organic Coconut Water
Hydration Base
The foundation of the formula.
Coconut water is one of nature's most effective hydration sources. Rich in potassium, magnesium, and natural electrolytes, it replenishes what you lose during performance without artificial additives found in traditional sports drinks.

Organic Beetroot Extract
Natural Energy
Nature's pre-workout.
Beetroot increases nitric oxide production, dilating blood vessels, improving oxygen delivery to muscles, and enhancing endurance — all without caffeine. Studies show it can improve athletic performance by 1–3% at every level of sport.
Nitric OxideEnduranceBlood FlowNatural Color
03
Organic Ashwagandha
Adaptogen
Fight fatigue. Not your nerves.
An ancient adaptogen used for thousands of years to reduce fatigue, support recovery, and improve physical performance. Unlike caffeine, it doesn't spike and crash — it helps your body adapt to stress and sustain energy over time. We use KSM-66, the most researched form. Safe for all ages including youth athletes.
Anti-FatigueRecoveryStress ResponseKSM-66
04
L-Theanine
Focus
Calm focus. Zero jitters.
An amino acid found naturally in green tea that promotes relaxed alertness. It sharpens focus without the anxious, jittery feeling that caffeine causes — ideal for athletes who need mental clarity during competition. Safe for all ages and one of the most studied amino acids for cognitive performance.
Mental ClarityNo JittersAmino AcidFocus
05
Vitamin B6 + B12
Energy Metabolism
Real energy from the inside out.
B vitamins are essential for converting food into usable energy at the cellular level. B6 supports protein metabolism and neurotransmitter production while B12 supports red blood cell formation and reduces fatigue — both critical for athletic performance. We use methylcobalamin (B12) — the most bioavailable form. Safe for all age groups.
Energy MetabolismAnti-FatigueMethylcobalaminAll Ages

Organic Lemon + Strawberry
Pink Lemonade Flavor
Flavor the right way.
Organic lemon juice concentrate and Meyer lemon extract form the tart, bright lemonade base. Organic strawberry extract adds natural sweetness and depth — no artificial flavors, no synthetic colors. Sweetened with organic cane sugar and organic monk fruit. Low sugar (8g), no stevia aftertaste.
